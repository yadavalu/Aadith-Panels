Projects in Berlin

Project 1: Solar Panel System Design and Installation for Radha-Govinda Temple

Radha-Govinda Temple is a famous Hindu Temple in Berlin, known for its vibrant festivals. These festivals however come with their cost: high electricity bills and high carbon footprint. Installing solar panels was the ideal solution to both reduce the costs as well as a fall in the carbon emissions.
